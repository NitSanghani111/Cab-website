Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a ride?

You can book a ride by calling us at (123) 456-7890, using our contact form, or emailing us directly. We're available 24/7 for bookings.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and digital payments. Corporate accounts are also available for business customers.

How far in advance should I book?

For immediate rides, we typically respond within 5-10 minutes. For scheduled rides, we recommend booking at least 2 hours in advance.

Do you provide airport services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive airport transfer services with flight tracking and meet & greet options available.
